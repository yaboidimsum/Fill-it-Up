Welcome to Fill it Up! ITS Edition, a simple yet challenging game where your objective is to fill the entire grid with a single color. Here's how it works:

On each turn, you have the power to change the color of the top-left square and all connected squares. Two squares are considered connected if they are adjacent and share the same color. By strategically choosing colors, you can gradually transform the entire grid into a uniform shade.

But beware! The computer player can be quite "greedy" in its color choices. Your ultimate goal is to outsmart the computer moves and demonstrate your skill. Can you triumph over its cunning strategies? Let the battle begin!
